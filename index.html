ABOUT THE RESTAURANT

This restaurant named Yuzie was owned by my father. His name is Che Azazi bin Mohd Saad. He run this restaurant for 24 years already. He started this when his first child was born, which is my older sister. He started together with my mom. The idea of the restaurant name is the actually the combination my parent's name.

Our humble restaurant was served a breakfast meals such as roti canai, nasi lemak , peknga/lempeng, roti jala and many more. We aslo served many types of beverages.
